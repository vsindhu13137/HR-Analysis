📊 HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

📌 Project Overview:
This project presents an HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze employee data and understand attrition patterns within the organization.

The dashboard helps HR teams identify:
Employee attrition trends,
Department-wise attrition,
Age & gender impact on attrition,
Salary distribution by job role,
Job satisfaction levels.

🎯 Business Problem-
Employee attrition affects organizational productivity and costs.

The objective of this dashboard is to:
Analyze attrition rate,
Identify high-risk departments,
Understand salary trends,
Examine satisfaction ratings,
Discover demographic patterns.

📈 Key Metrics
Total Employees: 1470
Active Employees: 1233
Attrition Count: 237
Attrition Rate: 16.12%
Average Salary Hike: 22K


-----
📊 Dashboard Insights
👥 Employee Demographics
Majority employees are in the 25–34 age group and
Gender distribution is balanced across age groups

🏢 Department-wise Attrition
Highest attrition observed in R&D
Sales department also shows significant turnover
HR has comparatively lower attrition

💰 Monthly Income by Job Role
Sales Executive earns the highest overall income (0.43M).
Research Scientist and Laboratory Technician follow.
HR and Research Director roles show lower total income.

📉 Attrition by Age & Gender
Higher attrition observed in younger employees (Under 35).
Gender-based attrition patterns visible across age groups.

⭐ Job Satisfaction
Satisfaction ratings range between 1–4.
Several employees show mid-level satisfaction (3–4).
